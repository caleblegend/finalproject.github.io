The Ministry of Sport is committed to fostering the growth and development of sports at all levels across the nation. We aim to inspire, empower, and provide opportunities for athletes and sports enthusiasts to achieve their full potential, while also promoting the values of teamwork, resilience, and health.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life through the promotion of sports and physical activities, fostering a culture of excellence, inclusivity, and participation.
