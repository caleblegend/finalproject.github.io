Soca Warriors Suffer Defeat by Saudi Arabia

Dwight Yorke was on the losing end in his first match as Trinidad and Tobago national senior team football coach, when the Soca Warriors were beaten 3-1 by Saudi Arabia in a closed-door friendly international in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia this morning, T&T time.

In a game arranged outside of the official FIFA match-window, T&T conceded a penalty in the 13th minute which was converted by Saleh Alshehri. The teams went to half-time with the Saudis ahead 1-0.

In the second period, the hosts doubled their lead through Abdullah Alhamddan in the 53rd minute and put the game away two minutes from the end of regulation time via Nasser Aldosari. However, T&T did eventually get on the scoresheet through substitute Jamal Jack in added-on time.
